## Summary

I built an AI agent that monitors job boards daily and emails you scored matches

## Content

Indeed keyword alerts send you 40 irrelevant jobs. We send you 10 that actually fit.

🎯 AI-scored matches GPT-4 reads each full job description and scores it 0–100 against your actual skills, experience level, and salary expectations — not just keywords.

💬 Explained in plain English "Strong React/TypeScript match, 5 years aligns well with the senior role, remote salary likely in range." You know exactly why each job made the list.

📈 Gets smarter over time Thumbs up or down on any match. The agent learns your real preferences — company size, industry, deal-breakers — beyond what your profile says.
